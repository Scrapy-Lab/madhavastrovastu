Recommended Gemstones

  • Ruby (Manikya): Known for enhancing leadership skills and self-confidence, Ruby is associated with the Sun and helps strengthen willpower and creativity.
  • Emerald (Panna): A gemstone of Mercury, Emerald promotes intellectual growth, communication skills, and boosts career prospects in business or creative fields.
  • Blue Sapphire (Neelam): Linked to Saturn, Blue Sapphire is known for removing negative energies and bringing about rapid success and protection from unforeseen dangers.
  • Yellow Sapphire (Pukhraj): Associated with Jupiter, Yellow Sapphire is known for bringing wealth, wisdom, and clarity in decision-making, especially in finances and relationships.
  • Red Coral (Moonga): Mars' gemstone, Red Coral, boosts courage, physical strength, and is recommended for those seeking protection against accidents and to overcome fear.
  • Diamond (Heera): Venus’ gemstone, Diamond enhances luxury, beauty, and relationships. It brings love, harmony, and prosperity to life.
  • Hessonite (Gomed): Linked to Rahu, Hessonite helps clear confusion, ward off negative influences, and improve financial stability.
  • Cats Eye (Lehsunia): Ketu's gemstone, Cats Eye is recommended for removing financial hardships and for spiritual advancement.
